Focus

From Scott's book

  • Problem 1: Failing to start focusing

    (aka, procrastinating).
    • 5 minutes rule: Whenever you find yourself procrastinating because you find it unpleasant, start with doing only 5 minutes.
    • Pomodoro: Once 5 minutes is too short and you can't keep doing it after 5, progress to the pomodoro technique: put an alarm after 25-30 mins and focus intensely for this time

  • Problem 2: Failing to sustain Focus

    (aka, getting distracted).
